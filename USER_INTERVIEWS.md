## Interview Strategy

**How to conduct:**
1. Find 3 target founders/eng leads via:
   - Cold DM on X/Twitter (look for those posting about AI tool costs)
   - Indie Hacker Slack communities
   - University/local tech networks
   - Friends of friends with relevant roles
2. DM: "I'm building a free AI spend audit tool for my internship project. Would love 15 min feedback on the concept?"
3. Schedule 10-15 min Zoom call
4. Ask:
   - "How many AI tools does your team use?"
   - "How much do you spend monthly?"
   - "Have you ever looked at optimizing this?"
   - "What would make this tool actually useful to you?"
   - "Would you use this?"
5. Record key verbatim quotes
6. Note surprising moments (best inputs for design)
7. Implement feedback immediately

**Success criteria:**
- Each interview uncovers 1 specific design change
- 3+ specific quotes per interview (not paraphrased)
- At least 1 "surprise" moment per interview (something you didn't expect)
- Average NPS implied from feedback

**Timeline:**
- Conduct interviews: Days 2-5 of development
- Implement feedback: Days 5-6
- Update this doc with real data: Day 7 (before submission)
