## FAQ (5 Real Q&As)

### 1. Does it really take 2 minutes?
**A:** Yes. It's 5 questions about your team and tools, then instant audit. No long forms, no callbacks, no sales pitch first.

### 2. Will you sell my email?
**A:** No. We collect email only if you request the report, and only to send the audit summary. Credex may reach out about high-savings cases—that's the whole point. We don't sell lists.

### 3. What if I already think I'm optimized?
**A:** Great—the audit will confirm that. We show you where you're doing well, not just where you're overspending. ~40% of audits show teams are already efficient.

### 4. Does this work for teams smaller than 3 people?
**A:** Yes. The audit works for 1 person up to 1000+. Recommendations scale by team size, so even solo founders get personalized advice.

### 5. Is this a Credex upsell?
**A:** It's a free tool by Credex to help founders optimize spending. If your audit shows big savings potential (>$500/month), we mention Credex credits as one way to capture that. But the audit is genuinely useful whether you use Credex or not.
